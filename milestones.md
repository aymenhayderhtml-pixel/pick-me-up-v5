# Pick Me Up Infinite Gacha Milestones

## Milestone 1: Readable Roster
- Hero names are canonicalized and consistent.
- Roster cards and detail panels share the same identity language.
- Role labels are easy to read on small portrait screens.

## Milestone 2: Meaningful Memorial
- Fallen heroes remain visible in the memorial layer.
- Echo progress is tracked and displayed.
- Memorial Hall feels emotionally distinct from the rest of the game.

## Milestone 3: Weekly Dungeon Rhythm
- Dungeon cards show weekly availability.
- The current-day dungeon list is clear at a glance.
- Start button availability matches the weekly schedule.

## Milestone 4: Stronger Economy
- Gold, Gems, Stamina, and upgrade materials all matter.
- Facility purpose is clearer in the UI and data model.
- Synthesis and upgrades create real spending pressure.

## Milestone 5: Premium Mobile Feel
- All major screens are portrait-first and thumb-reachable.
- Procedural UI feels polished, not placeholder.
- Transitions and confirmations feel deliberate.

## Milestone 6: Future-Ready Foundation
- Save/load compatibility is maintained as data grows.
- The codebase can accept new live-ops systems later.
- Core architecture stays stable while content expands.
