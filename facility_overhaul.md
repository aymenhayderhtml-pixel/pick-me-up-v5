# Facility Overhaul

## Goal
Turn facilities into the strategic backbone of the base, not just a set of passive upgrade buttons.

## Final Facility Structure

### Command Facilities
- `Workshop`
  - Crafts gear, consumables, and upgrade materials.
  - Handles legacy reforging from dead heroes.
  - Feels like the practical heart of the command center.

- `Square`
  - The morale hub.
  - Runs daily addresses, rallies, and morale choices.
  - Feels like the emotional pulse of the base.

- `Dorms`
  - Rest, recovery, bond events, and fatigue removal.
  - Converts downtime into attachment and lore.

- `Training Hall`
  - Active stat development, drills, and specialization.
  - Gates harder content through preparation.

- `Flying Dock`
  - Expedition planning, sortie launch, and deployment management.
  - Controls how the Master sends teams into the world.

### Shadow Facilities
- `Crucible`
  - Replaces the vague holding/prison concept with a clear sacrifice and promotion room.
  - Handles synthesis, promotion, and condemned-unit conversion.

- `Memorial Hall`
  - Archives fallen heroes and generates Echoes.
  - Converts loss into legacy bonuses and emotional weight.

- `Tactical Station`
  - Lets the Master intervene during dungeon runs with limited signals.
  - Turns the player into an active commander instead of a passive manager.

## Upgrade Loop
- Use Gold, Gems, Memorial Fragments, and facility materials.
- Upgrades should unlock mechanics, not just bigger numbers.
- One upgrade should be active at a time so the player must choose priority.
- Tier 1-2: core function expansion
- Tier 3: system unlock
- Tier 4: special command ability
- Tier 5: named legacy bonus or signature effect

## Core Rules
- Workshop must stay separate from sacrifice so the tone stays readable.
- Square and Dorms must affect morale visibly.
- Crucible must feel morally dangerous and mechanically powerful.
- Dock must have real consequence and sortie planning pressure.
- Memorial Hall must preserve fallen heroes as meaningful legacy, not dead content.

## Expected Result
- Facilities become a reason to log in daily.
- Loss feeds progress in a visible way.
- The base feels like a command center with emotional and strategic weight.
